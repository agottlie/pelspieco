ABOUT

It has been a delight and a challenge to bring fresh pies to Prospect Lefferts Gardens since March 27, 2015.

 

We have taught ourselves to bake better every day and grow with the palettes of the best set of regular customers in the city, and respond enthusiastically to that ever present question:

 

"What do I want to eat?"

 

Everything is made from scratch and even the most decadent of treats are wholesome. We offer the best coffee by Counter Culture Coffee and the best tea (in our giant mugs) by Kusmi Tea, and always try our best to bring local and exotic wine and beer to make your day even better.
